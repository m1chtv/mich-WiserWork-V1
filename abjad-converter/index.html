About Our Abjad Converter

Welcome to WiserWork Abjad Converter – your essential tool for calculating the Abjad value of Arabic text! Whether you're studying numerology, linguistics, or exploring Arabic gematria, our web-based converter makes it easy to calculate the numerical value of Arabic letters.

Simply input your Arabic text, and our tool will instantly calculate its Abjad value. You can also choose between the standard Abjad order or the Maghribi order, and optionally ignore lone Hamzah characters.

By default, the isolated hamzah (ء) is assigned a value of 1, similar to hamzah on alif. However, since this isn't universally agreed upon, you can choose to ignore it using the checkbox.

Key features:
- Calculate Abjad values for Arabic text.
- Toggle between standard and Maghribi order.
- Option to ignore lone Hamzah characters.

No complex formulas or manual calculations—our tool ensures accuracy and saves time.
