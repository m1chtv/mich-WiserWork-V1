Date Format Converter

This web page helps you effortlessly convert between date formats like Hijri (Islamic), Gregorian, and Shamsi (Khorshidi). Whether you're planning events, tracking dates, or working across cultures, this tool ensures accurate and easy calendar conversions.

How It Works:
This converter allows you to input a date in one of the following formats:

1.Hijri (Islamic): A lunar calendar used in many Muslim-majority countries.
2.Gregorian: The solar calendar widely used internationally.
3.Shamsi (Khorshidi): A solar calendar used in Iran and Afghanistan.

Once you provide a date in one format, the tool instantly converts it into the other two formats, giving you a clear understanding of the corresponding dates across different calendars.
