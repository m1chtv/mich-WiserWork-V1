How BMI Index Calculator Works

1. Weight (kg): Your body weight in kilograms.

2. Height (cm): Your height in centimeters.

Using these values, the calculator applies the standard BMI formula to provide your BMI index. The result is categorized into different ranges, such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obese, to help you interpret what your BMI means for your health.


Why Use This Tool?

Quick and Easy: Get your BMI in seconds with just two inputs.
Health Awareness: Whether your weight is in a healthy range for your height. Track Progress: Use the tool regularly to monitor changes in your BMI over time.
